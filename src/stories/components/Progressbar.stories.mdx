## Progressbar  standard
 <p>Different types of progress bar and you can customize the progress bar following steps</p>
 <ul>
 <li>change the aria-valuenow attribute with actual value to show the percentage of the progress</li>
 <li>change the style of "formsflow-progressbar-count" left to as per your requirements </li>
 <li>change the style of  formsflow-progressbar width to as per your requirements </li>
 </ul>
<div class="formsflow-progressbar-container formsflow-progressbar-standard"   >
    <div class="formsflow-progressbar-count" aria-valuenow="90%" style={{"left":"90%"}}></div>
     <div class="formsflow-progressbar">
       <div  style={{"width":"90%"}}></div>
    </div>
</div>
<br/>
