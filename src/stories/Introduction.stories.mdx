## Progress Indicators
progress bar indicates that the user's request has been received and the application is making progress toward completing the requested action.
<p>Given below different types of progress bar and you can customize the progress bar using following steps</p>
 <ul>
 <li>change the "aria-valuenow" attribute with actual value to show the percentage of the progress</li>
 <li>change the "left" attribute of "formsflow-progressbar-count"  as per your requirements </li>
 <li>change the "width" attribute  "formsflow-progressbar"  as per your requirements to increse the length of progress bar </li>
 <li>change the "height" attribute of "formsflow-progressbar-container" to increase the height of the progress bar </li>
 </ul>
 <br/>
<div class="formsflow-progressbar-container formsflow-progressbar-standard" style={{height:"2px"}} >
    <div class="formsflow-progressbar-count" aria-valuenow="90%" style={{"left":"90%"}}></div>
     <div class="formsflow-progressbar">
        <div style={{"width":"90%"}}></div>
    </div>
    <span>Uploading Files</span>
</div>
 <div className="subheading">Usage</div>

```html dark
<div class="formsflow-progressbar-container formsflow-progressbar-standard" style="height:2px" >

  <div class="formsflow-progressbar"><div style="width:90%"></div></div>
```
